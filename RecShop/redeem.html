How to Redeem

Follow these steps to activate your Steam keys instantly.

1

Open Steam Client

Launch the Steam application on your PC and log in to your account.

2

Navigate to Games

Click the "Games" menu at the top of the Steam window.

3

Activate Product

Select "Activate a Product on Steam..." from the dropdown menu.

4

Enter Your Key

Enter the key you received from RECSHOP and click Next. The game will be added to your library instantly!
